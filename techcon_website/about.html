About TechCon 2024

The History of TechCon

A group of tech enthusiasts formed TechCon in 2020 with the goal of establishing a forum for innovation and knowledge exchange. With thousands of guests each year, it has developed into one of the biggest technology conferences in Africa throughout the years.

The conference has evolved significantly over time to reflect the latest trends and technological advancements. With the rapid evolution of the tech industry, TechCon offers new formats and themes each year.

The TechCon Mission and Aims

TechCon aims to promote creativity, ease networking, and offer a forum for exchanging cutting-edge technological research and advancements. Our goal is to empower and instruct participants so they can use technology to address problems in the real world.

TechCon aims inclusivity, and promotes sustainable practices in the technology industry.
