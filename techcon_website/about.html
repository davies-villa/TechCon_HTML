About TechCon 2024

History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2020 by a group of tech enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for innovation and knowledge sharing. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most significant technology conferences in Africa, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

The conference has evolved significantly, adapting to the latest trends and technological advancements. Each year, TechCon introduces new topics and formats to keep up with the ever-changing tech landscape.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, facilitate networking, and provide a platform for sharing cutting-edge research and developments in technology. We strive to inspire and educate attendees, helping them to leverage technology to solve real-world problems.

At the heart of TechCon is a commitment to diversity, inclusion, and the promotion of sustainable practices within the tech industry.

Notable Past Speakers

Lionel Masimba

Lionel Masimba, often considered one of the first computer programmers, was a keynote speaker at first conference TechCon in 2020. He discussed the early history of computing and its future potential.

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, shared his vision for space exploration and sustainable energy at TechCon 2010. His talk inspired many attendees to think big and innovate.
